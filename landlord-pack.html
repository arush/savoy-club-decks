02 · Who We Are

A retailer built for the 21st century UK.

Savoy Club is a membership-led warehouse retailer for the UK's fast-growing multicultural communities. We combine world foods, trade & DIY, solar energy, furniture, a food hall, and free kids play under one 80–100k sq ft roof.

We are fully funded with £15M of committed Series A capital.

The Model
Membership warehouse club — think Costco, built for UK multicultural families. Higher dwell time, bigger baskets, strong loyalty.
The Opportunity
8M+ multicultural households with no dedicated destination. Fastest-growing consumer segment. Spending power rising year on year.
For You as Landlord
A well-capitalised, long-term anchor tenant with high footfall, strong community pull, and a globally proven format.
