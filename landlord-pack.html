02 · Who We Are

A retailer built for the 21st century UK.

Savoy Club is a new-format, membership-led warehouse retailer designed to serve the UK's fast-growing multicultural communities — and everyone who shops alongside them.

We combine world foods, trade & DIY, solar energy, furniture, a food hall, and free kids play under one premium 80–100k sq ft roof. Nothing like this exists in the UK today.

We are fully funded to execute, with £15M of committed Series A capital, an experienced retail operations team, and a clear rollout plan for 10+ sites nationally.

The Model
Membership warehouse club — think Costco, but built for UK multicultural families. Higher dwell time. Higher basket size. High loyalty.
The Opportunity
8M+ multicultural households in the UK with no dedicated destination. Fastest-growing consumer segment. Spending power rising year on year.
For You as Landlord
A well-capitalised, long-term anchor tenant with high footfall, strong community pull, and a proven global format adapted for the UK.

05 · Site Requirements

What we need. What we bring.

What We're Looking For
📐Size: 80,000–100,000 sq ft GIA, ideally on a single trading floor. Mezzanine or ground-floor + lower ground also considered.
🚗Parking: Minimum 300+ spaces, ideally 400–600. Free customer parking essential. Disability access compliant.
🛣️Location: Motorway-adjacent or ring-road catchment preferred. Easy arterial access. 10-minute drive radius population 200k+.
🏗️Ceiling height: 6m+ clear eaves for warehouse racking. Industrial or retail warehouse format ideal.
🔌Power: 3-phase power, 500kVA+ supply. Cold room and freezer room infrastructure preferred or buildable.
🚚Delivery access: Minimum 4 loading bay doors. HGV access essential. 24hr delivery access preferred.
🌱Sustainability: Solar roof access a bonus. EPC C or better preferred. EV charging capable.
Ideal Formats
Former Retail Warehouse
Ex-Costco, B&Q, Homebase, or similar large-format box. Already configured for warehouse retail.
Industrial/Distribution Unit
Conversion to premium retail possible with our fit-out. We invest heavily — the space becomes premium on our own capital.
Brownfield / Anchor Redevelopment
Willing to engage on new-build or shell-and-core deals for the right location. Pre-let discussions welcome.
